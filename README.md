## What is Buddy?

Buddy is an **AI-powered trading bot** designed specifically for **Pump.fun** on the Solana blockchain. It monitors new token launches in real-time and makes intelligent trading decisions using advanced algorithms and pattern recognition.

Unlike simple bots that rely on basic filters, Buddy uses a sophisticated **Predator Engine** that analyzes multiple signals to find the best entry opportunities.

## Getting Started

### 1. Activate Your License

When you first open Buddy, you'll see a welcome screen asking for your license key.

- Enter your license key
- Click "Activate"
- You're ready to go!

### 2. Configure Your Wallet

Go to **Settings** and enter:

- **Public Key** - Your Solana wallet address
- **Private Key** - Your wallet's private key (kept locally, never sent anywhere)

> Your private key is stored locally on your computer and is only used to sign transactions.

### 3. Adjust Settings

Configure your trading parameters:

| Setting | Description | Recommended |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Buy Amount | SOL per trade | 0.05 - 0.1 SOL |
| Stop Loss | Max loss % | 15-20% |
| Take Profit | Target multiplier | 2x - 3x |
| Trailing Stop | Follow price up | 15% |
| Max MC | Maximum market cap | $30,000 |
| Slippage | Transaction slippage | 25% |

### 4. Start Trading

- **Simulation Mode** - Toggle ON to test without real trades
- **Live Mode** - Toggle OFF to trade with real SOL

Click **Start Agent** and Buddy will begin monitoring Pump.fun!
